On this day...

Sepia tones and heavy stalks my leaves kept me clothed

Nought but fate had it

On this day

Your rays glistened through my leaves, the warmth i felt

Your smile i saw, your laugh i heared

Helpless to your winds caress my leaves fell on this day

...

What worlds lie beneath these hazel eyes?

What wit hid beneath that infectious smile?

On this day i thought

Purposeless before, but i shall float no more

On this day I stood

A naked tree cast amongst my amber leaves,

On this day I knew

Tomorrow shall be green and what wonderous colour!

For the brush that colours my soul
